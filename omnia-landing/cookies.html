Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences and enabling certain functionality.

2. How We Use Cookies

Omnia AI uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function properly
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your language and display settings
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our website
  • Performance Cookies: Improve website speed and functionality

3. Types of Cookies We Use

3.1 Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the website to function and cannot be switched off. They include:

  • Session management cookies
  • Security and authentication cookies
  • Language preference cookies

3.2 Functional Cookies

These cookies enhance functionality and personalization:

  • User interface preferences
  • Display settings (dark/light mode)
  • Previously visited pages

3.3 Analytics and Performance Cookies

We use these cookies to understand website usage and improve performance:

  • Page visit statistics
  • User interaction data
  • Error reporting and debugging

4. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are set by third-party services we use:

  • Vercel Analytics: Website performance monitoring
  • Google Fonts: Web font delivery

5. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Block All Cookies: Disable all cookies (may affect functionality)
  • Block Third-Party Cookies: Only block external service cookies
  • Delete Existing Cookies: Remove cookies already stored
  • Notification Settings: Get notified when cookies are set

6. Browser-Specific Instructions

Chrome

Settings → Privacy and Security → Site Settings → Cookies and site data

Firefox

Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data

Safari

Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data

Edge

Settings → Site permissions → Cookies and site data

7. Impact of Disabling Cookies

Disabling cookies may affect:

  • Language preferences (will reset to default)
  • User interface customizations
  • Login session management
  • Website performance optimization

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of our service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Information

For questions about this Cookie Policy, please contact us through our official support channels or website.

By using Omnia AI, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to this Cookie Policy.
