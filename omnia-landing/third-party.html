Third-party Services

Last updated: January 2025

1. Overview

Omnia AI integrates with various third-party services to provide you with the best possible AI experience. This page discloses all external services we use, their purposes, and links to their privacy policies.

2. AI Models & Providers

Omnia AI leverages multiple AI models to provide diverse and powerful capabilities:

Anthropic (Claude Models)

Purpose: Advanced reasoning, analysis, and conversational AI

Data Shared: Your messages and conversation context

Privacy Policy: https://www.anthropic.com/privacy

OpenAI (GPT Models)

Purpose: Natural language processing and generation

Data Shared: Your messages and conversation context

Privacy Policy: https://openai.com/privacy/

Google (Gemini Models)

Purpose: Multimodal AI capabilities and analysis

Data Shared: Your messages, images, and files

Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. Infrastructure & Storage

Supabase

Purpose: Database management and file storage

Data Shared: Your account information, conversations, and uploaded files

Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

Vercel

Purpose: Web hosting and content delivery

Data Shared: Website usage data and analytics

Privacy Policy: https://vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy

4. Voice & Audio Services

ElevenLabs

Purpose: Text-to-speech and voice generation

Data Shared: Text content for voice synthesis

Privacy Policy: https://elevenlabs.io/privacy

5. Analytics & Performance

Vercel Analytics

Purpose: Website performance monitoring and optimization

Data Shared: Anonymized usage statistics and performance metrics

Privacy Policy: https://vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy

6. Font & UI Services

Google Fonts

Purpose: Web font delivery for improved typography

Data Shared: Basic request information (IP address, user agent)

Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

7. Data Processing & Privacy

When you use Omnia AI:

  • Your data may be processed by the above third-party services
  • We implement appropriate safeguards and data processing agreements
  • Data sharing is limited to what's necessary for service functionality
  • You can delete your data at any time through your account settings

8. International Data Transfers

Some third-party services may process data outside your country of residence. These services implement appropriate safeguards such as:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
  • Adequacy decisions by relevant authorities
  • Industry-standard security measures

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access information about third-party data processing
  • Opt out of non-essential third-party services
  • Request deletion of your data from our systems
  • Contact us with questions about third-party integrations

10. Updates to Third-party Services

We may add, remove, or change third-party services to improve Omnia AI. When we make significant changes, we will update this page and notify users as appropriate.

11. Contact Information

For questions about third-party services or data processing, please contact us through our official support channels or website.

By using Omnia AI, you acknowledge that your data may be processed by the third-party services listed above in accordance with their respective privacy policies.
